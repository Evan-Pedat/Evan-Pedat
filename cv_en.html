I especially enjoy the feeling of speed and the serene beauty of snow-covered mountains. Skiing is not just a sport; it’s also a way to build strong bonds with those who share the same passion. No matter where life takes me, the slopes will always be a special place for me.

When I was young, my father, who loved fishing, would often take me to the riverside. We would spend hours casting our lines, enjoying the peace of the water and the surrounding nature.
